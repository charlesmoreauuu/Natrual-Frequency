Modal analysis — bending mode shapes

Generalized eigenvalue problem K·φ = ω²·M·φ on a 60-element Euler-Bernoulli beam with consistent mass matrix. Foundation springs and RNA mass included. Solved by inverse iteration with M-orthogonal deflation.

Methodology

PISA. Four-component Winkler model (distributed lateral p, distributed moment m, base shear H_B, base moment M_B) linearised at zero displacement per DNV-ST-0126 §3.6.2.4 (elastic soil behaviour). Pile = Euler-Bernoulli beam, N elements. Soil distributed springs lumped at nodes by tributary length. Base springs concentrated at the pile-tip node. Mudline stiffness extracted by applying unit force and unit moment at the head, inverting the resulting 2×2 flexibility matrix. Nominal coefficients: Dunkirk dense sand (Burd et al. 2020), Cowden stiff till (Byrne et al. 2020).

NFA — first mode (Rayleigh). Stepped cantilever from mudline to hub per DNV-ST-0126 §3.6.2. Above-mudline pile = 1 segment; tower = N segments from DB tower profile. Per-node EI and mass per unit length at ~0.5 m resolution. Stiffness: k_str = 1/δ_tip, where δ_tip is from double-integration of curvature κ(z) = M(z)/EI(z) under a unit tip force (proper stepped-beam result). Modal mass via Rayleigh: M_eq = M_RNA + I_RNA × (θ_tip/δ_tip)² + ∫ m(z) ψ²(z) dz, with ψ = δ/δ_tip. I_RNA is Ixx (fore-aft) or Iyy (side-to-side) from Table 9-1. Added hydrodynamic mass ρ_w × π/4 × D_sub² (Ca = 1.0) and marine growth mass over submerged length, where D_sub = D + 2 × t_mg. Series compliance with foundation springs: 1/k_eq = 1/k_str + 1/K_lat + L²/K_rot. f₁ = (1/2π) √(k_eq / M_eq).
NFA — higher modes (eigenvalue). 60-element Euler-Bernoulli beam; consistent mass matrix; foundation springs at base; RNA translational mass at tip (rotary inertia not included in higher modes). Solved by inverse iteration with M-orthogonal deflation. DNV §3.6.2.3 requires the highest computed mode ≥ 1.2 × 3P = 0.464 Hz.

Resonance margins. DNV-ST-0126 §3.6.2.2: fR/f0,n ≤ 0.95 or ≥ 1.05 (5 % minimum). Natural frequencies varied by ±5 % per §3.6.2.5 to account for mass/stiffness uncertainties; both bounds must satisfy the margin requirement.
SWL envelope. NFA is recomputed at the 50-yr positive (+3.16 m) and negative (−2.71 m) still-water levels (Table 10-3); margins use worst-case f₁ across the envelope.
Wave fp band. Period band from Tmin (THmax,lower at 1-yr return) to Tmax (Tp at 100-yr return) per Table 10-1/12-2. Overlap with the soft-stiff window triggers a warning.
VIV check. Strouhal vortex-shedding frequency fs = St × U / D, with U = 50-yr surface current (Table 10-4) and D = above-mudline pile OD at waterline. Required by DNV-ST-0126 §3.6.2.6 and §3.6.3.3. fs within ±10 % of f₁, or inside the soft-stiff window, triggers a warning.
