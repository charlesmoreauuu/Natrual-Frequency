Methodology

PISA. Four-component Winkler model (distributed lateral p, distributed moment m, base shear H_B, base moment M_B) linearised at zero displacement. Pile = Euler-Bernoulli beam, N elements. Soil distributed springs lumped at nodes by tributary length. Base springs concentrated at the pile-tip node. Mudline stiffness extracted by applying unit force and unit moment at the head, inverting the resulting 2×2 flexibility.

NFA. Stepped cantilever from mudline to hub. Above-mudline pile = 1 segment; tower = N segments interpolated from the DB tower doc profile (section 4 above). Per-node EI and mass per unit length computed at ~0.5 m resolution. Stiffness: k_str = 1/δ_tip, where δ_tip is from double-integration of curvature κ(z) = M(z)/EI(z) under a unit tip force (proper stepped-beam result, not length-weighted EI). Modal mass via Rayleigh: M_eq = M_RNA + ∫ m(z) ψ²(z) dz, with ψ = δ/δ_tip (static-deflection mode shape - energy-consistent, replaces 33/140 × m_dist). Added hydrodynamic mass ρ_w × π/4 × D² and marine growth mass applied over submerged length. Series compliance with PISA mudline springs: 1/k_eq = 1/k_str + 1/K_lat + L²/K_rot. f₁ = (1/2π) √(k_eq / M_eq).

SWL envelope. NFA is recomputed at the 50-yr positive and negative still-water levels (Table 10-3); margins use the worst-case f₁ - lowest f₁ versus 1P, highest f₁ versus 3P.
Wave fp band. Period band from Tmin (THmax lower at 1-yr) to Tmax (Tp at 100-yr) per Table 10-1. Overlap with the soft-stiff window triggers a warning.
VIV check. Strouhal vortex-shedding frequency fs = St × U / D, with U = 50-yr surface current (Table 10-4) and D = above-mudline pile OD. Required by DNV-ST-0126 §3.6.3. fs within ±10 % of f₁, or inside the soft-stiff window, triggers a warning.
